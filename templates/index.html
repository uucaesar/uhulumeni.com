Products Covered

Renewable Energy Products
With the growing demand for sustainable power solutions in South Africa, renewable energy products are subject to strict NRCS regulations to ensure safety, performance, and compatibility with the national electrical grid. Compliance is especially important for imported products to guarantee they meet local technical standards.
  • Solar Inverters – devices that convert DC power from solar panels into usable AC electricity for homes and businesses.
  • Solar & Portable Chargers – charging units for residential, commercial, and mobile applications.
  • Battery Systems - lithium-ion, lead-acid, and other energy storage solutions used in backup power and off-grid setups.
Consumer Electronics
Consumer electronics include everyday devices used for communication, entertainment, and personal convenience. These products must comply with NRCS electro-technical regulations to ensure they are safe, reliable, and compatible with South African electrical systems.
  • Televisions – LED, OLED, and smart TVs in various sizes and resolutions.
  • Audio Equipment – speakers, amplifiers, home theatre systems, and soundbars.
  • Power Supplies & Chargers – AC/DC adapters, USB chargers, and portable charging devices.
  • Small Electronic Devices – radios, set-top boxes, and other plug-in or battery-powered gadgets.
Household Appliances
This category includes a wide range of everyday electrical products used in homes, all of which must comply with NRCS electro-technical safety and performance standards before they can be sold or imported into South Africa.
  • Kettles – electric kettles, cordless kettles, and hot water dispensers.
  • Microwaves – countertop and built-in models with various power outputs.
  • Refrigerators - single-door, double-door, bar fridges, and chest freezers.
  • Washing Machines - top-loaders, front-loaders, and washer-dryer combos.
Commercial Equipment
This category covers heavy-duty electrical products designed for industrial, manufacturing, and commercial use. Due to their scale and power consumption, these products are subject to strict NRCS regulations to ensure they are safe, energy-efficient, and compliant with South African technical standards.
  • Industrial Ovens – large-capacity electric ovens used in manufacturing, food processing, and laboratories.
  • Compressors – air and gas compressors for factories, workshops, and commercial facilities.
  • HVAC Systems – commercial-grade heating, ventilation, and air conditioning units for offices, warehouses, and retail spaces.
Lighting Products
Lighting products are closely regulated by the NRCS to ensure they meet South African safety, performance, and energy-efficiency standards. These regulations help protect consumers, reduce electricity consumption, and maintain product quality in the market.
  • LED Bulbs – energy-efficient light bulbs for residential, commercial, and industrial use.
  • Fluorescent Lights – tube and compact fluorescent lamps (CFLs) used in offices, factories, and retail environments.
  • Fixtures & Fittings – lamp holders, light fittings, and complete lighting assemblies for both indoor and outdoor applications.
