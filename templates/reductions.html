Terms and Conditions

1. Enquiry & Payment

An enquiry fee of R50 is required to check for pending traffic tickets. Your enquiry will only be processed once proof of payment is received. We only take online payments.

2. Refunds

All payments are non-refundable under any circumstances, including but not limited to, no tickets found after enquiry, client cancellations, municipal system delays or failures.

3. Ticket Reduction

One ticket will be reduced free of charge. Each additional ticket reduction is charged at R50 per ticket. Reductions are processed only after full payment for selected tickets is received.

4. Processing Time

Reduced tickets will be sent to the client within 7 working days from payment confirmation. While Uhulumeni strives to meet these timelines, delays caused by municipal systems are beyond our control.

5. Client Responsibility

Clients must provide accurate details (ID number or number plate). Any incorrect or incomplete details provided may affect the results and Uhulumeni will not be held liable.

6. Liability

Uhulumeni is not responsible for the issuance of traffic tickets or the accuracy of municipal records. Our role is limited to assisting with ticket enquiries and reductions. We cannot guarantee a specific percentage or amount of reduction, as this depends on municipal systems and regulations.

7. Confidentiality

All client information and documents submitted will be treated as strictly confidential. Information will be used only for the purpose of processing traffic ticket enquiries and reductions.

8. Accuracy

Uhulumeni retrieves ticket information exclusively from the PayCity database. Most tickets listed on PayCity are also flagged during roadblocks, but not all tickets may be loaded on the PayCity database at the time of enquiry. Therefore, Uhulumeni does not guarantee that we will find all your tickets, and cannot be held liable for any tickets missed.
